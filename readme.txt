Receive a readable email summary of WordPress Site Health results.

== Description ==

MozCheck periodically runs WordPress Site Health checks and sends an HTML email
with critical and recommended issues, available updates, inactive plugins, and
unused themes.

Choose a weekly or monthly schedule, multiple recipients, report categories,
and notification conditions such as critical issues, update availability, or a
worse result than the previous scheduled check.

WP-Cron runs when the site receives traffic, so an email may be delivered later
than its configured time.
